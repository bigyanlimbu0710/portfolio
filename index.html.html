NITRO V15

INFO

The Acer Nitro V 15 is a gaming laptop that combines powerful performance with portability. It features up to an Intel Core 7 processor and a GeForce RTX 5060 GPU, making it suitable for fast-paced gaming and multitasking. With a 1080p display and a 180Hz refresh rate, it offers a smooth visual experience. The laptop is designed for efficiency, with Max-Q technology that enhances battery life while maintaining performance. Overall, the Nitro V 15 is a great choice for gamers looking for a budget-friendly option with solid performance.
